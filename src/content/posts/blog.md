## why blog?

Journaling and blogging are the private and public sides of the same coin. It's just that for the latter the intended audience is more than the single person that is you.

My writing and self-reflection skills have undoubtedly grown stronger with my extensive journaling. With blogging, constant revisions are necessary to make sure you can be understood by the most people.
